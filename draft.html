G. Accessibility, responsive, and AI notes
  • Semantic tags: header/nav/main/footer; aria-label on nav menus; alt text for all media.
  • Contrast-aware palette; focus states on buttons/links; skip-to-content link optional.
  • Responsive: grids collapse to single column under 900px; fluid images; clamp for hero text.
  • CSS3 features: gradients, shadows, hover transforms, transitions, rounded corners.
  • Media: min 3 types (images, video, audio) and a click-to-swap gallery.
AI usage: initial content prompts and layout ideas were drafted with AI then customized for copy, navigation, validation flow, and accessibility cues. Final code and styling will be authored manually.
H. Page-by-page notes
  • Home (index.html): First impression page; states business name, short pitch, hero CTA, and quick highlights that guide visitors to modules and contact.
  • About (about.html): Origin story, mission, and principles; builds trust by showing experience, accessibility stance, and support approach.
  • Modules (services.html): Explains the product features (attendance, gradebook, analytics, messaging, wellbeing, audits) with tags for key capabilities and an invite to book a walkthrough.
  • Media (media.html): Visual proof of the platform; thumbnail grid that swaps a main image, plus embedded video/audio to demonstrate flows.
  • Contact (contact.html): Provides direct contact details, social links, location visual, and a validated form with user feedback for demo or enquiry requests.
