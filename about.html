Mission & principles

Data with empathy

We believe every data point represents a student. Reports are designed to spark meaningful conversations, not just dashboards.

Accessibility first

Alt text on imagery, labeled controls, clear focus states, and ARIA hints make the platform usable for everyone.

Privacy & safety

We minimize data collection, encrypt in transit and at rest, and never resell information. Parents stay in control of how they are contacted.

Responsive support

Live chat, onboarding playbooks, and local training sessions ensure staff are confident from day one.

Implementation approach

Step 1

Setup & sync

Import rosters securely, map bell schedules, and configure attendance codes to mirror your existing policy.

Step 2

Staff onboarding

Short role-based guides for teachers, leaders, and office staff plus hands-on practice environments.

Step 3

Launch & review

Measure attendance trends and grade submissions in the first month, then adjust automations to fit your workflow.
